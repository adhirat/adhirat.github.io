Experience Spiritual Growth with Gauranga

The Gauranga app, developed by Adhirat Technologies, is designed specifically for ISKCON devotees to assist and enrich their spiritual journey. Available for Android, iOS, macOS, and web, it is free to use and has many exciting features planned for future updates.

Audio Gallery

Listen to a collection of spiritual lectures, bhajans, and kirtans anytime, anywhere.

Video Gallery

Watch videos that inspire devotion and provide insights into Vedic teachings.

Image Gallery

Explore a beautiful collection of spiritual images for meditation and reflection.

E-Books Gallery

Access a digital library of sacred texts and spiritual books at your fingertips.

Quiz Gallery

Test your knowledge with fun and engaging quizzes to deepen your understanding.

Sadhana Card

Track and monitor your daily spiritual practices to stay motivated on your path.

Japa Counter

Keep count of your mantra chanting with an easy-to-use digital japa counter.

Community Builder

Connect with other devotees and participate in group discussions and events.

Available on Multiple Platforms

The Gauranga app is accessible on Android, iOS, macOS, and the web, allowing you to use it on your preferred device.
