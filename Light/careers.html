How We Hire

We've designed our hiring process to be transparent, fair, and respectful of your time. We want to get to know the real you, not just what's on your resume.

1

Application Review

Our team carefully reviews every application to assess alignment with the role and our values.

2

Initial Screening

A casual chat with our recruiter to discuss your experience, goals, and interest in Adhirat.

3

Technical & Culture Fit

Deep dive into your skills with the team, followed by a conversation about our values and how we work.

4

The Offer

We extend an offer and welcome you to the team to start building the future together.
